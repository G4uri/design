origami pencil stand

how to make it :

Step 1 : Cut a color paper sheet and lay it flat on a table. .

Step 2 : fold the paper, making it into different shapes using origami.

Step 3 : make 6 of these and glue them together.

Step 4 : glue a paper bottom to the pen/pencil stand so that nothing falls out

use :

These origami pencil stand are so easy to make and they are perfect for an at home work station, or throw some magnets on the back and and use them in lockers to hold pencils and pens! They are so easy to make and can change their look in so many different ways depending on the paper you use.

advantages :

A stylish pen stand helps you to keep all your writing tools and accessories organized and easily accessible. 2. A decorative pen stand for study table is available in a wide range of options that upgrade the style quotient of your office desk or wherever you keep them.

disadvantages :

A paper pen/pencil stand is made out of paper so if it comes in contact with water, it will not be able to keep its shape and not become as stable, which could cause the stationary inside it to fall.
